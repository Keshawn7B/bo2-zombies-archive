# Contributing knowledge

## Entry rules

1. Use a unique lowercase `id` with hyphens.
2. Keep the summary useful during a match: one or two sentences.
3. Put nuance, setup steps, and exceptions in `details`.
4. Distinguish verified game behavior from community-tested hidden mechanics.
5. Avoid claiming an exact trigger or rank formula unless the source is reproducible.
6. Do not add copyrighted screenshots or extracted game assets without permission.

## Preferred categories

- Maps
- Weapons
- Perks
- Buildables
- Enemies
- Persistent Upgrades
- Mechanics
- Strategies
- FAQ

## Review checklist

- Search finds the entry by its normal name and alternate name.
- Pack-a-Punch names are spelled correctly.
- Point costs specify solo/co-op differences when applicable.
- Map-specific claims identify the relevant map or mode.
- Hidden mechanics include a confidence label or caveat.
